Cafe GoodLuck is a Irani cafe-restaurant on the Fergusson College Road, in the Deccan Gymkhana area of Pune

One of the most iconic breakfast spots in the city, we celebrates the good old days. We are amongst the oldest Irani places and perhaps the first garden restaurant in Pune. Cafe GoodLuck is known for its bun maska/bun omelette and Iranian tea.
